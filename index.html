About

A bit about me

Hey there, I'm Kavan — a network and infrastructure technician with a background that spans telecom field work, HFC plant maintenance, and software development.

Currently at Rogers Communications as an HFC Plant Maintenance Technician, I handle everything from RF signal troubleshooting and outage resolution to NOC coordination, ServiceNow ticketing, and planned change control across the BC Interior.
